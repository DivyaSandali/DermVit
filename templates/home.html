Skin Diseases We Specialize In

Enfeksiyonel (Skin Infections)

Infectious skin diseases caused by bacteria, viruses, fungi, or parasites, leading to conditions like rashes, redness, or irritation.

Eczema

A skin condition that causes redness, itching, dryness, and sometimes swelling. It’s common, not contagious, and can flare up due to triggers like allergens or stress.

Benign Tumor

A non-cancerous growth that does not spread to other parts of the body. It’s usually harmless but may require attention if it causes discomfort or other issues.

Malign Tumor

A cancerous growth that spreads uncontrollably to other parts of the body, potentially becoming life-threatening if not treated promptly

About Us

Welcome to DermViT, a cutting-edge system developed to help detect skin
diseases with accuracy and efficiency. This project focuses on identifying four
key conditions: Infectious skin diseases, Eczema, Benign tumors, and
Malignant tumors.

DermViT leverages advanced artificial intelligence technology to provide
reliable and fast predictions, empowering users to take timely action for their
skin health. Designed to be user-friendly, this system is here to make skin disease detection accessible and convenient for everyone.

Our goal is simple: to support early diagnosis and improve overall skin health through innovative technology.

At DermViT, we believe that early detection saves lives. By combining
state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms and an extensive dataset of dermatological conditions, our system is equipped to analyze images with remarkable precision. This ensures that users receive accurate insights and
actionable recommendations for their skin health.

Our platform is not only tailored for individual users but also serves as a valuable tool for healthcare professionals. Doctors and dermatologists can leverage DermViT's analysis to supplement their expertise, ensuring more informed
decision-making and better patient outcomes.

FAQs

How can I use DermViT?

Simply upload a clear image of the affected skin area through DermViT’s interface, and the system will analyze it to provide a prediction.

Yes, DermViT is designed as a supportive tool. It’s always recommended to consult a dermatologist for a professional diagnosis and treatment plan.

Is DermVit's detection system accurate?

Yes, DermVit's detection system is designed to deliver high accuracy, achieving an overall accuracy of 9248% in identifying skin conditions. Our advanced Vision Transformer (ViT) model is trained on a carefully balanced and enhanced dataset to ensure reliable predictions for the four key skin disease types. While no system is perfect, DermVit prioritizes precision and reliability to provide trustworthy results.

Is my data safe with DermViT?

Absolutely. DermVit prioritizes your privacy and data security. Any images or information you provide are processed securely and are not stored or shared. DermVit operates with strict adherence to data protection standards, ensuring that your data is used solely for generating predictions and is promptly discarded after processing.
